Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #2C3E50 – Used for header background, navigation bar, footer, buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #ECF0F1 – Used for body background, content sections, form inputs.
  • Accent Color: #723704 – Used for call-to-action buttons, hover states, icons.

Site Plan Overview

This site plan outlines the structure, design, and functionality of the Oval Marketplace project, which will feature a company directory, product listings, and advertising opportunities.

Site Purpose

The Oval Marketplace website aims to provide a comprehensive platform for businesses to showcase their products and services. It will serve as a trusted directory for users seeking reliable companies and offer advertising spaces for businesses to promote their brands effectively.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A small business owner wants to list their company and products to reach new customers and increase sales. How easily can they register and showcase their offerings?
  • Scenario 2: A customer is looking for trusted companies to purchase specific products. How quickly can they find product listings and contact information?

Typography

  • Primary Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif – Used for body text, navigation, paragraphs.
  • Secondary Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – Used for headings, hero banners, section titles.

Wireframes

Mobile View

  • Header with logo and hamburger menu
  • Hero section with search bar and featured products
  • Company directory section with cards for each company
  • Advertising section with grid layout for ads
  • Footer with contact info and social media links

Desktop View

  • Header with logo, navigation menu, and search bar
  • Hero section with large banner image and featured products
  • Company directory section with grid layout for companies
  • Advertising section with larger ad banners
  • Footer with detailed contact info and social media links

CSS Guidelines

  • Mobile-first responsive design approach will be used.
  • Typography and color scheme will follow the defined standards above.
  • Navigation will be responsive with a hamburger toggle on smaller screens.
  • Semantic HTML and reusable class-based CSS will be applied consistently.
